# English Study Curriculum (A1 → C2) – 50 Lessons

---

## A1 – Beginner

### Lesson 1  
**Title:** Introduction to English  
**Content:** Alphabet, basic pronunciation  
**Outcome:** Recognizes English letters and sounds.

### Lesson 2  
**Title:** Greetings and Introductions  
**Content:** Hello, goodbye, names  
**Outcome:** Introduces oneself politely.

### Lesson 3  
**Title:** The Verb "To Be"  
**Content:** am / is / are (affirmative)  
**Outcome:** Forms basic sentences.

### Lesson 4  
**Title:** "To Be" Questions and Negatives  
**Content:** question forms, not  
**Outcome:** Asks and answers simple questions.

### Lesson 5  
**Title:** Personal Pronouns  
**Content:** I, you, he, she, it, we, they  
**Outcome:** Refers to people correctly.

### Lesson 6  
**Title:** Possessive Adjectives  
**Content:** my, your, his, her  
**Outcome:** Expresses ownership.

### Lesson 7  
**Title:** Numbers and Age  
**Content:** numbers 1–100, asking age  
**Outcome:** Uses numbers in context.

### Lesson 8  
**Title:** Days and Months  
**Content:** calendar vocabulary  
**Outcome:** Talks about dates.

### Lesson 9  
**Title:** Present Simple (Affirmative)  
**Content:** daily routine verbs  
**Outcome:** Describes habits.

### Lesson 10  
**Title:** Present Simple (Negative & Questions)  
**Content:** do / does  
**Outcome:** Asks about routines.

---

## A2 – Elementary

### Lesson 11  
**Title:** Adverbs of Frequency  
**Content:** always, usually, sometimes  
**Outcome:** Talks about frequency.

### Lesson 12  
**Title:** There is / There are  
**Content:** singular and plural forms  
**Outcome:** Describes places.

### Lesson 13  
**Title:** Prepositions of Place  
**Content:** in, on, under, next to  
**Outcome:** Describes location.

### Lesson 14  
**Title:** Countable and Uncountable Nouns  
**Content:** some / any  
**Outcome:** Talks about quantity.

### Lesson 15  
**Title:** Past Simple (Regular Verbs)  
**Content:** -ed forms  
**Outcome:** Describes past actions.

### Lesson 16  
**Title:** Past Simple (Irregular Verbs)  
**Content:** common irregular verbs  
**Outcome:** Talks about past events.

### Lesson 17  
**Title:** Past Time Expressions  
**Content:** yesterday, last week  
**Outcome:** Uses time references.

### Lesson 18  
**Title:** Comparatives  
**Content:** -er / more  
**Outcome:** Makes comparisons.

### Lesson 19  
**Title:** Superlatives  
**Content:** -est / most  
**Outcome:** Expresses extremes.

### Lesson 20  
**Title:** Future with Going To  
**Content:** plans and intentions  
**Outcome:** Talks about future plans.

---

## B1 – Intermediate

### Lesson 21  
**Title:** Future with Will  
**Content:** predictions and decisions  
**Outcome:** Expresses future ideas.

### Lesson 22  
**Title:** Present Continuous  
**Content:** verb + ing  
**Outcome:** Describes actions happening now.

### Lesson 23  
**Title:** Present Continuous vs Present Simple  
**Content:** tense comparison  
**Outcome:** Chooses correct tense.

### Lesson 24  
**Title:** Past Continuous  
**Content:** was / were + ing  
**Outcome:** Describes ongoing past actions.

### Lesson 25  
**Title:** Modal Verb Can / Could  
**Content:** ability and requests  
**Outcome:** Expresses ability politely.

### Lesson 26  
**Title:** Modal Verb Must / Have To  
**Content:** obligation  
**Outcome:** Talks about rules.

### Lesson 27  
**Title:** First Conditional  
**Content:** real future situations  
**Outcome:** Talks about possible results.

### Lesson 28  
**Title:** Zero Conditional  
**Content:** general truths  
**Outcome:** Expresses facts.

### Lesson 29  
**Title:** Linking Words  
**Content:** and, but, because  
**Outcome:** Connects ideas.

### Lesson 30  
**Title:** Paragraph Writing  
**Content:** topic sentence, support  
**Outcome:** Writes clear paragraphs.

---

## B2 – Upper Intermediate

### Lesson 31  
**Title:** Present Perfect  
**Content:** experiences and results  
**Outcome:** Talks about life experiences.

### Lesson 32  
**Title:** Present Perfect vs Past Simple  
**Content:** tense contrast  
**Outcome:** Uses correct past tense.

### Lesson 33  
**Title:** Passive Voice (Basic)  
**Content:** be + past participle  
**Outcome:** Forms passive sentences.

### Lesson 34  
**Title:** Reported Speech (Statements)  
**Content:** tense changes  
**Outcome:** Reports information.

### Lesson 35  
**Title:** Relative Clauses  
**Content:** who, which, that  
**Outcome:** Gives detailed descriptions.

### Lesson 36  
**Title:** Second Conditional  
**Content:** unreal situations  
**Outcome:** Talks about imagination.

### Lesson 37  
**Title:** Opinion Essays  
**Content:** structure and language  
**Outcome:** Writes organized essays.

### Lesson 38  
**Title:** Discussion Language  
**Content:** agreeing and disagreeing  
**Outcome:** Participates in discussions.

---

## C1 – Advanced

### Lesson 39  
**Title:** Advanced Vocabulary  
**Content:** academic and abstract words  
**Outcome:** Expresses ideas precisely.

### Lesson 40  
**Title:** Advanced Passive Forms  
**Content:** passive with modals  
**Outcome:** Uses complex passive structures.

### Lesson 41  
**Title:** Reported Questions and Commands  
**Content:** indirect speech  
**Outcome:** Reports complex speech.

### Lesson 42  
**Title:** Complex Sentences  
**Content:** conjunctions and clauses  
**Outcome:** Writes fluently.

### Lesson 43  
**Title:** Formal Writing  
**Content:** reports, formal emails  
**Outcome:** Writes professionally.

### Lesson 44  
**Title:** Presentation Skills  
**Content:** structure and clarity  
**Outcome:** Delivers clear presentations.

---

## C2 – Proficiency

### Lesson 45  
**Title:** Idioms and Expressions  
**Content:** advanced idiomatic language  
**Outcome:** Understands natural English.

### Lesson 46  
**Title:** Register and Tone  
**Content:** formal vs informal usage  
**Outcome:** Adjusts language to context.

### Lesson 47  
**Title:** Critical Reading  
**Content:** analyzing complex texts  
**Outcome:** Evaluates written material.

### Lesson 48  
**Title:** Advanced Speaking  
**Content:** debates and arguments  
**Outcome:** Speaks fluently and confidently.

### Lesson 49  
**Title:** Writing with Style  
**Content:** clarity, nuance, coherence  
**Outcome:** Writes at near-native level.

### Lesson 50  
**Title:** Full Proficiency Review  
**Content:** integrated skills practice  
**Outcome:** Uses English effectively in all situations.
